tournament()¶

Tournament selection. Two individuals are selected to enter into the tournament. The individual with the higher fitness score has a winRate chance of winning the tournament, while the individual with the lower fitness score has a (1 - winRate) chance of winning tournament. The tournament winner goes into the gene pool
